About Me

Just do it!

As a Foreign Language major, I was often questioned about why I chose to work as a website designer and developer. However, English, the language I majored in, is a tool for me. I am also interested in design and programming. My goal is to become a T-shaped person. I have at least three years of industry experience in website design, covering a wide range of fields such as gaming, finance, agriculture, leather goods, satellite technology, body care products, and job banks, with detailed information included in the work experience section. In my leisure time, I teach myself web design through online lectures, continuously practicing and seeking answers. Additionally, I enjoy taking on freelance jobs to challenge myself in different industries. As a student of foreign literature and language, this experience is incredibly helpful and significant to my career, allowing me to gain knowledge about the world, including website design, more quickly. I love to gather information from different cultures. "Passion matters a lot!" and "Stay hungry, stay foolish" are proverbs that encourage me to become a better person.
